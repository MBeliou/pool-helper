Crystal-clear water, without the guesswork.

My Pool turns a water test into an exact, step-by-step plan for balancing your pool — tailored to its size, surface, and chemistry. No spreadsheets, no chemistry degree, no more dumping in chemicals and hoping.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Log a water test — enter your strip or kit readings. My Pool instantly flags anything out of range.
2. Get your dosing plan — see the precise amount of each product to add, calculated from your pool's real volume.
3. Follow the steps — add, re-test, and you're balanced.

EVERYTHING YOU NEED
• Guided water tests with per-parameter units
• Exact dosing plans based on your pool's real volume
• Diagnose cloudy, green, or foamy water and follow a clear fix plan
• Trends over time, so you catch issues before they start
• Smart re-test reminders on a cadence that fits your pool
• Works for pools and hot tubs, chlorine or salt

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your pool data lives on your device. No account required, nothing sold, nothing tracked. You can export everything at any time.
